## Furnace Pricing Formula (Net 35%)

```
Selling Price = ((Equipment + Thermostat + Materials) × 1.10 + $1,800) × 1.08675 ÷ 0.65
```

### What each factor covers
| Factor | Amount | Purpose |
|--------|--------|---------|
| × 1.10 | 10% | Tax on parts and materials |
| + $1,800 | flat | Labor |
| × 1.08675 | 8.675% | 5% commission + 3.5% credit card fee |
| ÷ 0.65 | — | Target 35% net profit after all costs |

### Defaults (use unless specified otherwise)
- Thermostat cost: **$100**
- Materials cost: **$322**

### Example
Equipment cost = $2,500 | Thermostat = $100 | Materials = $322

`($2,500 + $100 + $322) × 1.10 + $1,800 = $4,862.20`
`$4,862.20 × 1.08675 ÷ 0.65 = **$8,123** selling price`

## System Pricing Formula (General — 40% Gross Margin)

```
Selling Price = (Bundle Cost × 1.095 ÷ 0.60) + Labor
```

### What each factor covers
| Factor | Amount | Purpose |
|--------|--------|---------|
| × 1.095 | 9.5% | Tax + credit card + misc fees |
| ÷ 0.60 | — | 40% gross margin |
| + Labor | varies | Added flat after margin calc |

### Labor by job type
| Job Type | Labor |
|----------|-------|
| Full split system (AC + furnace) | $2,040 |
| Mini-split (single zone) | $500 |
| Mini-split (customer-supplied unit) | $900 labor only |

### Round up to nearest $10 after calculation.

- Do NOT include price, cost, or install time in listings unless customer requests it
- Format: structured bullets, simplified homeowner language
